This large marine turtle is not truly a dinosaur, but a reptile. Its shell is like that of a leatherback turtle — thick and rubbery rather than being formed of solid plates. With its powerful front flippers, the archelon is a fast swimmer; it seldom ventures on to dry land except to spawn.
